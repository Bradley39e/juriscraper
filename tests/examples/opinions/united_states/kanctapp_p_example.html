About published and unpublished opinions

Published opinions

The Kansas Supreme Court usually releases its published opinions around 9:30 a.m. on Fridays. These opinions are posted as soon as possible after they are released. Opinions posted online may not be final. A party may request a rehearing, modification, or further review, or the court may determine a change is needed. Final opinions are published in the Kansas Reports.  Citing opinions

The Kansas Court of Appeals usually releases its published opinions around 9:30 a.m. on Fridays. These opinions are posted as soon as possible after they are released. Opinions posted online may not be final. A party may request a rehearing, modification, or further review, or the court may determine a change is needed. Final opinions are published in the Kansas Court of Appeals Reports.  Citing opinions

 

Unpublished opinions

 The Kansas Supreme Court usually releases its unpublished opinions around 9:30 a.m. on Fridays. These unpublished opinions are posted online the same day they are released. Unpublished opinions posted online may not be final. A party may request a rehearing, modification, or further review. Unpublished opinions do not get published in the Kansas Reports.
